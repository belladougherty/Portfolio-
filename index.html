Projects

Percent Change in Household Income and Median Rent (2014–2019)

Analyzed socioeconomic trends in San Diego County using U.S. Census data. Mapped percent change in household income and median rent between 2014 and 2019 to highlight patterns of urban inequality and inform policy decisions. Created detailed thematic maps using GIS.

Urban Heat Island in Seattle

Designed an in-depth research poster exploring the Urban Heat Island effect in Seattle. Used GIS to analyze impervious surfaces, tree canopy, parks, car emissions, and temperature anomalies to support urban planning.

Suitable Areas for Big Foot - Washington State

Developed a habitat suitability model for Washington State using GIS analysis of land cover and environmental variables. Produced clear, layered maps highlighting areas with highest potential suitability.
